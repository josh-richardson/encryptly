About Us

Encryptly aims to deliver secure online chat to large numbers of people in a way which is transparent and easy for the user. Because of the fact that all of the encryption and key generation is done inside your browser rather than on our servers, Encryptly can deliver its service for free. Encryptly was built in part due to the revelation of government sponsored spying, and in part due to regimes in both the USA and the UK cracking down on encryption and encryption related tools. Encryptly gives peace of mind that your communications are not being read by any third parties.
